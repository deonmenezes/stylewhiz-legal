StyleWhiz AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026 · Applies to the StyleWhiz AI iOS app (bundle ID com.deonmenezes.stylewhiz)

Section 1 — Overview

StyleWhiz AI is a personal styling app. We collect only what we need to generate AI styling looks for you and to run your account. We never sell your personal data, never use it for advertising or tracking, and never use it to train AI models.

Section 2 — Data We Collect and How We Collect It

We collect the following data, always directly from you inside the app:

  • Account data — your email address and display name, provided when you sign up.
  • Style preferences — gender, age range, and style "vibes" you select during onboarding, plus styling attributes (skin undertone, body type) detected from your photo.
  • Photos (selfies) — the selfie photographs you deliberately capture or choose from your photo library. See Section 3.

We do not collect location, contacts, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or any data from third-party sources.

Section 3 — Face Data: What We Collect

StyleWhiz AI collects only the selfie photographs you choose to upload — standard two-dimensional photos. We do not collect facial geometry, face meshes, faceprints, depth maps, or any biometric identifiers, and we never perform facial recognition or use your photo to identify you. A photo is only ever captured or selected by you; the app never collects photos automatically or in the background.

Section 4 — Face Data: How We Use It

Your photo is used solely to:

  • Generate AI virtual try-on images — composing your chosen catalog outfit, makeup, or accessory onto your photo so you can preview the look; and
  • Detect styling attributes — skin undertone and body type — to personalize outfit recommendations.

There are no other uses. Your photos are never used to train AI models, never sold, never shared with data brokers, and never used for advertising, tracking, or identification.

Section 5 — Face Data: Sharing With Third Parties and Where It Is Stored

To deliver the try-on feature, your photo is shared with exactly two parties, and only after you give explicit permission in the app (see Section 8):

  • Our servers (Amazon Web Services, United States) — your photo is uploaded over HTTPS and stored in private Amazon Web Services (S3) storage in the United States so you can keep generating looks without re-uploading.
  • Google’s Gemini AI service — when you request a try-on, your photo and the chosen catalog product image are transiently processed by Google’s Gemini generative model solely to synthesize your try-on image. Google processes your photo under data protections equal to or stronger than ours and does not use it to train its models, retain it beyond processing, or use it for any other purpose.

No other third party ever receives your photo or face data.

Section 6 — Face Data: Retention and Deletion

Your photo is retained only while your account remains active. You can replace your photo at any time from Profile. Deleting your account in Profile → Delete Account permanently removes your account, your photos, and all styling data from our servers. Photos sent to Google’s Gemini service are processed transiently and are not retained by Google after your try-on image is generated.

Section 7 — Storage and Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS). Photos and account data are stored in access-controlled private cloud storage (Amazon Web Services, United States). Access is restricted to the systems that operate the styling features described above.

Section 8 — Your Explicit Permission and Your Choices

StyleWhiz AI never uploads or shares your photo without asking first. Before your first upload or try-on, an in-app permission screen explains exactly what is shared (the selfie you chose and the catalog outfit image), who receives it (our Amazon Web Services servers and Google’s Gemini AI service), and how long it is kept. Nothing is sent if you decline. You can withdraw consent at any time by replacing or removing your photo, or by deleting your account in Profile.

Section 9 — Children’s Privacy

StyleWhiz AI is not directed at children. Do not upload photos of minors. If we learn that a child’s data has been uploaded, we will delete it.

Section 10 — Changes to This Policy

If we change how we handle your data, we will update this policy and the in-app copy, and material changes to photo handling will require your renewed permission in the app.
